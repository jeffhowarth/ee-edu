# __parent materials of soils__  

## __purpose__  

Your goal is to make a map that could help town planners in Addison County compare information from the Addison County soil survey to property parcels in their town.  

Practically, your workflow will include the following patterns:  

* gather and inspect vector data  
* filter feature collections  
* convert vector to raster  
* mask raster data  
* filter and flatten an image collection  
* make terrain layers 
* paint strokes    

When you solve the problem, you should have a workflow that uses the town of Middlebury as the subject to produce a map with the layers shown in the app below. Ideally, you will write the script so that you can very easily shift the map to any other town in Addison County, Vermont.
